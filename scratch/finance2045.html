10 High-Impact Finance Themes

Curated topic tracks spanning the full spectrum of Indonesia's financial transformation journey.

01Digital Banking & NeobankingNavigating the rise of digital banks, BaaS, embedded finance, branch banking evolution, and building digital-first customer experiences at scale.
02AI & Emerging TechnologiesAgentic AI in banking, fraud detection and prevention, intelligent automation, data-driven financial products, and advanced risk analytics.
03Payments Innovation & Cross-Border FinanceQRIS & ASEAN payment interoperability, digital wallets, cross-border settlements, stablecoins, and the future of real-time payments.
04SME & MSME FinanceDigital credit solutions for 65M+ MSMEs, working capital optimisation, supply chain finance, UMKM Go Digital, and ERP-integrated lending.
05Financial Inclusion & Banking the UnbankedReaching 77M+ unbanked adults through alternative lending, alternative credit scoring, digital expansion strategies, and last-mile financial access.
06Halal Finance & Shariah InnovationCapitalising on the growth of Sharia banking, Sukuk markets, Islamic fintech, Sharia-compliant digital products, and Islamic capital markets.
07Enterprise Banking & Corporate FinanceModernising corporate treasury, B2B payments, ISO 20022 adoption, composable banking, enterprise embedded finance, and supply chain financing.
08Digital Assets & BlockchainNavigating institutional crypto adoption, real-world asset tokenisation, CBDC developments (Project Garuda), blockchain settlement infrastructure, and DeFi.
09Regulation, Policy & ComplianceAdapting to the OJK regulatory roadmap, digital banking licensing, RegTech, KYC/AML innovation, and regulatory sandboxes.
10Sustainable Finance & ESGPrioritising green bonds, climate risk in banking, ESG investment frameworks, sustainable capital markets, and energy transition finance.
