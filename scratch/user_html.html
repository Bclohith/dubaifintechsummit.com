H.E. Essa Kazim

Governor
DIFC
UAE

His Excellency Essa Kazim is the Governor of Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC), Chairman of Borse Dubai, Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Legislation Committee in Dubai, and a member of the Dubai Supreme Fiscal Committee. He plays a central role in shaping Dubai’s financial ecosystem, capital markets strategy, and legislative frameworks. Known for his leadership in financial market development and regulatory governance, H.E. Kazim brings a strategic perspective on global financial centres and market infrastructure.

Noel Quinn

Chairman of the Board of Directors
Julius Baer Group
UK

Noel Quinn is the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Julius Baer Group, with over 37 years of experience in global banking and financial services. He previously served as Group Chief Executive of HSBC, where he led the organization for five years and guided its global strategy and transformation. Prior to this, he held senior leadership roles including CEO Global Commercial Banking, Regional Head of Commercial Banking Asia Pacific, and Head of Commercial Banking UK. Known for his strategic leadership in global banking, Quinn brings deep insights into international finance and institutional growth.

Jenny Johnson

Chief Executive Officer
Franklin Templeton
USA

Jenny Johnson is the Chief Executive Officer of Franklin Templeton, one of the world’s largest global investment managers with $1.7 trillion in assets under management. With a career spanning more than 35 years, she has been a central force in transforming the firm into a diversified platform across public and private asset classes. Before becoming CEO in February 2020, she held senior leadership roles across investment management, distribution, technology, operations, and wealth management. Under her leadership, Franklin Templeton has expanded its global capabilities through strategic acquisitions and investments, strengthening its position as a leading multi-asset investment manager.

Tan Su Shan

Chief Executive Officer & Director
DBS Group
Singapore

Ms Tan Su Shan is the Chief Executive Officer and Director of DBS Group, Southeast Asia’s largest bank. With over 35 years of experience across consumer banking, wealth management, and institutional banking, she previously served as Deputy CEO and Group Head of Institutional Banking, and earlier led Consumer Banking and Wealth Management for nearly a decade. She has also held leadership roles at Morgan Stanley, Citibank, and ING Barings. Recognized globally, she ranked 6th on Fortune’s Most Powerful Women in Business list in 2025 and was featured on Forbes Asia’s Power Businesswomen list in 2024.

Kentaro Okuda

President & Chief Executive Officer
Nomura Holdings, Inc
Japan

Kentaro Okuda is the President and Group Chief Executive Officer of Nomura Holdings and President of Nomura Securities, and serves as a member of the Board of Directors of Nomura Holdings. With a long-standing career at Nomura since 1987, he has held senior leadership roles across global investment banking, including Global Head of Investment Banking, Global Head of M&A, and Joint Head of Wholesale. Previously, he served as Group Co-COO and President & CEO of Nomura Holding America, leading the firm’s Americas business. Known for his leadership in global capital markets and investment banking, Okuda brings deep insights into international financial markets and institutional strategy.

Ryan Breslow

Founder & Chief Executive Officer
Bolt
USA

Ryan Breslow is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Bolt, a leading checkout technology company reimagining how people buy, sell, and move money through one-click commerce and crypto-enabled payments. He has founded and led multiple ventures across fintech, digital commerce, and decentralized finance, including Love.com, Prism, Eco, and The Family Accelerator. Known for building next-generation payment and financial platforms, Breslow brings a founder-led perspective on the future of commerce, fintech, and digital money.

Shri Ashishkumar Chauhan

Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer
National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
India

Shri Ashishkumar Chauhan is the Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer of the National Stock Exchange of India (NSE), with decades of experience in capital markets, technology, and financial infrastructure. A founding member of NSE, he has been instrumental in shaping India’s modern markets, including screen-based trading, the NIFTY index, derivatives markets, and digital trading platforms. Previously, he served as MD and CEO of BSE Limited and held leadership roles across Reliance Group and other institutions. Recognized globally for his impact on financial markets innovation, he is regarded as a pioneer of modern financial derivatives in India.
